First-Time Patient Medical Clearance

To comply with Arizona state regulations and ensure the safety and well-being of our clients, Stay Dripped IV requires all first-time patients to complete a Good Faith Exam (GFE) before receiving IV therapy treatments.

The GFE is a streamlined 3–5 minute telehealth assessment performed over video call from the comfort of your home. Once completed, your clearance is valid for 12 months unless significant changes to your medical history occur.

How It Works:

  • Quick 3–5 minute video call
  • Valid for 12 months
  • Personalized care tailored to your wellness needs
